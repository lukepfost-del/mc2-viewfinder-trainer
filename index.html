MC2 Viewfinder Trainer

Practice aiming the MC2 emitter using your phone camera and a printed QR target that simulates the cassette detector.

  1. Print qr-target.pdf at 100% scale (NOT "fit to page"). Verify the 9 cm ruler is exactly 9 cm with a real ruler.
  2. Lay the page flat — that's your cassette.
  3. Tap Start, allow camera access, and aim your phone like the MC2 emitter.
Camera access requires HTTPS. Hold phone 30–80 cm from the target.
