MC2 Viewfinder Trainer

Practice aiming the MC2 emitter using your phone camera and a printed QR target that simulates the cassette detector.

  1. Print qr-target.pdf at 100% scale (NOT "fit to page"). Verify the 9 cm ruler with a real ruler.
  2. Lay the page flat on a table — that's your cassette.
  3. Tap Start, allow camera access, and aim your phone like the MC2 emitter.
  4. Watch the field indicator, center mark, SID and SSD gauges respond like the real Viewfinder.
Camera access requires HTTPS or localhost. For best results, hold phone 30–80 cm from the target.
